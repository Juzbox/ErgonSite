Understanding Ergon



At Ergon we believe there is work for everyone. As a central desk for work in the Eindhoven region, we help everyone to find work who is currently on social security or has a work disability. With our work fit program we ensure that people are (again) ready for the labor market.

Ikat is a dyeing technique used to pattern textiles that employs resist dyeing on the yarns prior to dyeing and weaving the fabric. It is also craft that has been adopted in several indian states and southeastern countries, each contributing their own nuances to the craft.
